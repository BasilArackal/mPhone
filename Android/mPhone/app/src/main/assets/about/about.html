About Us

mPhone founded in 2011, is an implied manufacturer, importer, exporter & distributor of innovative smart products & services. Since our inception, we have been at the forefront of creating a superior smartphone experience and have partnered with industry leaders including Intel®, NVIDIA®, Broadcom, Mediatek, AMD, Microsoft and Qualcomm®. mPhone a premium and classic smart devices brand, has progressed to become a market leader in the smart device market. With our own setup, the Product Range caters to every segment of population, starting with entry level Feature Phones to High-End 3G & 4G Smart Phones.
